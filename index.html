Welcome to VirtuCare

VirtuCare is a simulation designed to help healthcare professionals practice patient interviews in a realistic, low-pressure environment. You'll be introduced to one of two virtual patients. The patients, thier symptoms, their and responses may vary per session.

Before continuing, you'll need to allow microphone access to speak to the patient. Make sure to ask questions and gather information, just like you're in a real-world setting.

About

VirtuCare is designed to help students practice patient interviewing skills in a realistic, low-risk environment. Each session presents unique virtual patients and scenarios, encouraging clinical reasoning and natural conversation. This project is free to use and provided as-is for educational purposes.

The simulation and associated videos were created and developed by Dominique Thomas.
All character animations were rendered using Unreal Engine's MetaHuman framework.
